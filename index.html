Tissue Fusion

Led team in design, testing, and clinical testing of laser based medical device used to fuse tissue together. The device delivers laser energy while measuring biological tissue temperature in real-time using a feeback loop to control laser power. Information is provided to the physician via a LCD display intergrated into the system. Interlock safe guards are implemented in both hardware and firmware.
